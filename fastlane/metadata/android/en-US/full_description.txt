<p>No-cloud Android baby monitor for LAN and VPN use.</p>
<p>Open Babyphone allows two Android devices to act as a baby monitor. The first device, left in the room with the baby, advertises itself on the network and streams audio to a connected client. The second device, with the parent, connects to the monitoring device and receives the audio stream.</p>
<p>The project is an independent fork of Child Monitor, which itself is a fork of Protect Baby Monitor.</p>
<p>Open Babyphone works on Android 5.0 (Lollipop) and newer, i.e. Android SDK 21.</p>
