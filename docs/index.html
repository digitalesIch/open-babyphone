Open Babyphone

Local-network Android baby monitor without cloud accounts or remote access.

Android 11+ GPLv3 No cloud

Open Babyphone turns two Android devices into a direct audio monitor for use at home. One device stays near the child and streams microphone audio; the other stays with the parent and plays the stream.
